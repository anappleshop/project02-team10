Company Info

DrinkIn is an e-commerce international drinks collection. The company surfaces a variety of drinks from around the world. These are specifically non-alchologic drinks.


The ideal customer is 20-40 years old, adventurous, and enjoys trying new things.


This company offers nostaglic global drinks to customers in the US and Canada, which are not normally available or easily accessible. It also caters towards customers who wants to try new drinks.


The company is an online retailer that provides order convenience of international drinks.


Customer Profile

Font Selection

DM Serif Display was selected for the title copy due to its strong serifs which creates a unique brand for the company.


Inter was selected for it's easy-to-read san-serif type, which creates visual contrast from the serifs in DM Serif Display.




Colour Palettes

Blue is the primary colour. Chosen to stand out from major international drink colours (green, red, etc.) to create a distinct brand identity. Blue is used for main components, such as primary and secondary buttons, navigation, etc.


Light Blue is used as an acent colour. This colour is used to support the primary blue – light blue is used in hover states, and image treatment.


Brown is the website’s negative colour. Used to contrast with the primary blue in acent and in negative connotations, such as reset.


Grey is used to lower the hierarchy of text. Specifically, to show disabled interactions or labels.


Black is the main text colour. Used for the body copy and most headers.




Imagery and Art Direction

Call drinks for sale have their images on a blue-gradient background. This helps differentiate each drink, while keeping them consistent in style.
