About us

Drinkin was founded by David Mai and Monica De Mesa in 2022. Our company is a one stop shop for all refreshing and bubbly drinks. Our drink selection includes all sorts of popular international and exotic drinks. You can shop with us online and have the drinks delivered right to your door step.

Our mission

At Drinkin we strive to offer either familiarity or new discovery to our customers through a sip of a refreshing beverage. Our company provides easy access for our customers in the US and Canada to purchase nostaglic global drinks which are not normally available or easily accessible. We wish to help our customers to rediscover an old favourite or encourage them to discover a new favourite drink.
