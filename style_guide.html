Company Info

DrinkIn is an e-commerce international drinks collection. The company surfaces a variety of drinks from around the world. These are specifically non-alcoholic drinks.


The ideal customer is 20-40 years old, adventurous, and enjoys trying new things.


This company offers nostaglic global drinks to customers in the US and Canada, which are not normally available or easily accessible. It also caters towards customers who wants to try new drinks.


The company is an online retailer that provides order convenience of international drinks.


Customer Profile

Font Selection

DM Serif Display was selected for the title copy due to its strong serifs which creates a unique brand for the company.


Inter was selected for it's easy-to-read san-serif type, which creates visual contrast from the serifs in DM Serif Display.




Colour Palettes

Green is the primary colour. Chosen to stand for the vibrance shown in many major international drink colours. to create a distinct brand identity that represents the refreshing feeling of delicious drinks. Green is used for main components, such as primary and secondary buttons, navigation, etc.


Light Green is used as an accent colour. This colour is used to support the primary green.


White is the website’s negative colour. Used to contrast with the primary green in accent and in negative connotations, such as reset.


Grey is used to lower the hierarchy of text in the footer. As well it is used when hovering over in-text links.


Black is the main text colour. Used for the body copy and most headers.




Imagery and Art Direction

All drinks for sale have their images on a grey-gradient background. This helps differentiate each drink, while keeping them consistent in style.
