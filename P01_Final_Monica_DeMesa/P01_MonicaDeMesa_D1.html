PARAGRAPHS

‘Saya Merienda’ is from the tagalog (Filipino) language and means happy snack or fun snack. At Saya Merienda we provide karaoke rooms and a selection of classic filipino snacks to munch on. Our snack selection includes everything from canned mango juice to roller coaster cheese chips.

Our karaoke song selection books are filled with songs from many genres. Some genres that are included are R&B, pop, country, rap, soul and many more. As well, included in our modern day selection of songs are also lots of throwbacks. Come into Saya Merienda to enjoy some Merienda (snacks) and have a Saya (fun) time.

BRANDING ELEMENTS

About The Brand

What is Saya Merienda? Saya Merienda is a filipino owned Karaoke business which offers karaoke room services and Filipino snacks.

Who is the company to potential customers and who does the company cater to? A hip place that values creating fun memories with good company. People who love karaoke or are looking for a fun activity to do with their friends or family. Those who take pride in the Filipino culture and or want to share it with others.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service? It offers a fun activity for people looking for something to do on the weekend or a random weekday with their friends or family. As well for Filipinos it is a place that feels like home due to the familiar Filipino snacks. For others it is an exciting opportunity to enjoy karaoke with snacks that come from a culture that loves to sing.
